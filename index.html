My Passionate Hobby: Cooking

My opinion is that cooking is the best hobby to have, because it can be very useful in life.
Cooking is my hobby and has been for several years. I was about five years old, when I started learning
how to cook.



There are so many reasons I love to cook, cooking is one of those things that just comes naturally for me,
and I think I inherited, because I can cook almost anything and nail it on the first try,
much like my mother. But the main reason I love to cook is because it keeps me stress free.
For example when I am cooking I have very little time to think,
because the cooking is challenging my mind and taking my mind off the stress of everyday life.
